Place a bet and choose the number of rounds for the game. The more rounds, the less risky the game is, otherwise fewer rounds, increases the chances of earning more expensive money.

At the start of the game, the computer generates a file with a selection of ordered cards chosen at random.

Each round you must choose between two cards: King or Queen. You must guess which card the computer has chosen.

Each round of the game has a round value which is the bet amount divided by the number of rounds. If you guess it correctly, you earn the round value, but if you guess it wrongly, you lose it.
